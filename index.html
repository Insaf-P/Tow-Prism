About Us

Based out of Bengaluru - India, 2 PRISM is a consulting firm providing Technology, Business Development (BD), Recruitment and Training solutions to organizations in the semiconductor industry. We work with OEM's, Tier 1's and Tier 2 companies in the Embedded & IoT, Automotive, Industrial Automation and Agri Tech segments.
Our team brings in a collective experience of over 4 decades in the above areas curating and executing:

  • Cutting edge technology solutions

  • Aggressive go to market plans

  • Perennial skilled feet on ground

for our clients fostering their business.
